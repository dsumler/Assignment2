Steps:
1) Place sugar and water in a small saucepan; bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, 2 minutes. Cool completely.
2) Stir in lemon peel and juice. Freeze according to manufacturer's directions.
3) Transfer sorbet to a freezer container, allowing headspace for expansion. Freeze until firm, about 4 hours.
