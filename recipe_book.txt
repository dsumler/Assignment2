1) Chocolate Brownies

	Ingredients

		Dairy
			1 cup butter

		Fruit
			2 medium bananas

		Oils
			1 teaspoon vanilla extract

		Carbohydrates
			2 cups white sugar

		Grains
			1/2 cup cocoa powder
			1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

		Protein
			4 eggs

		Additives
			1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
			1/2 teaspoon salt

		Nuts
			1/2 cup walnut halves

	Steps

		1) Melt the butter or margarine and mix all ingredients in the order given.
		2) Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 to 30 minutes in a 9 x 13 inch greased pan.


	Tip: Aluminum foil can be used to keep food moist, cook it evenly, and make clean-up easier.

2) Lemon Cheesecake

	Ingredients
		
		Dairy
			50g butter	
			350g mascarpone cheese

		Fruit
			4 lemons (3 juiced, 1 zested)

		Carbohydrates
			25g light soft brown sugar
			75g caster sugar
		
		Sweets
			110g digestive biscuits

	Steps

		1) To make the base, crush 110g digestive biscuits in a food bag with a rolling pin or in the food processor.
		2) Melt 50g butter in a saucepan, take off heat and stir in 25g light soft brown sugar and the biscuit crumbs.
		3) Line the base of a 20cm loose bottomed cake tin with baking parchment. Press the biscuit into the bottom of the tin and chill in the fridge while making the topping.
		4) Beat together 350g mascarpone cheese, 75g caster sugar, the zest of 1 lemon and juice of 2-3 lemons, until smooth and creamy.
		5) Spread over the base and chill for a couple of hours.		

	Quoted from: "https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/1567660/lemon-cheesecake"


3) Blackberry Cobler

	Ingredients

		Fruit
			1 tablespoon lemon juice
			4 cups fresh blackberries, rinsed and drained

		Starch 
			2 tablespoons cornstarch
			
		Carbohydrates
			1 1/2 cups white sugar, divided

		Grains
			1 all-purpose flour

		Additives
			1 teaspoon baking powder
			1/2 teaspoon salt

		Liquids
			1/4 cup boiling water
			1/4 cup cold water


	Steps

		1) Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
		2) In a large bowl, mix the flour, 1/2 cup sugar, baking powder, and salt. Cut in butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in 1/4 cup boiling water just until mixture is evenly moist.
		3) In a separate bowl, dissolve the cornstarch in cold water. Mix in remaining 1 cup sugar, lemon juice, and blackberries. Transfer to a cast iron skillet, and bring to a boil, stirring frequently. Drop dough into the skillet by spoonfuls. Place skillet on the foil lined baking sheet.
		4) Bake 25 minutes in the preheated oven, until dough is golden brown.
